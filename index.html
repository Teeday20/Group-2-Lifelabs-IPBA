EDUCATIONAL VIDEOS

WHAT ARE EDUCATIONAL VIDEOS?

Educational videos are videos that teach people some things like science, math and other sorts of things. Educational videos can help a child learn better and easier and they are very important. Educational videos are very important since students will be able to understand them easier. It's also better since the students can keep repeating the video so if they are ever confused they could rewind it or pause to be able to keep up with the lesson.

TARGET AUDIENCE

OUR TARGET AUDIENCE

Our target audience would be our fellow students.

NEEDS

WHAT ARE THE NEEDS OF OUR AUDIENCE?

The needs of our audience would be a stable wifi connection and a device with the app “Youtube” installed or can access Youtube.com in a web browser in order to be able to access Education videos. Education videos address the need for people to gain knowledge. For us, our needs to make education videos would be a recorder, editing skills, and topics to talk about.
